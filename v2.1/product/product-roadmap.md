# Product Roadmap

## Sections

### 1. Marketing Foundation
Build the Astro shell (homepage, shared layout, Tailwind+daisyUI theme) and reusable marketing components to anchor the site.

### 2. Projects & Solutions Showcase
Develop the projects listing and detail cards that highlight Para, Scoold, cloud offerings, and other flagship products with clear CTAs.

### 3. Content Hub & Blog
Implement the Markdown blog system (collections, tags, pagination, feeds) and surface teasers across the site.

### 4. Support, Contact & Trust
Ship contact/support touchpoints, legal pages, and trust signals (status link, company details) so prospects can engage confidently.

### 5. SEO & Performance Infrastructure
Add structured data, metadata automation, responsive imagery, lazy loading, and Bun + GitHub Actions pipelines to meet SEO/perf goals.
